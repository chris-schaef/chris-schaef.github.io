Patrick Schaefer


Actor-Singer-Pianist




About


Patrick Schaefer is a musical theatre performer and pianist residing in Columbus,Ohio. As a graduate of Otterbein University (2012), Patrick moved to New York City to begin the frist couple years of his career. He returned to his stomping grounds, where he began landing leading roles in productions around Columbus. Patrick is proud to announce that he is a Actor's Equity Membership Candidate.

As someone who has spent the majority of his life playing piano, Patrick loves playing almost as much as he loves singing. He is more than happy to play at weddings and other ceremonies if asked to do so. Furthermore, Patrick has a history of writing. Inspired by Weird Al Yankovic, at age 14 Patrick became the youngest artist played on the Dr. Demento show.
